STEMtelling connecting student lives to STEM classrooms and the world

STEMtelling creates personalized storytelling experiences that centers high school students’ interests, cultures and experinces as the foundation for scienctific innovation and exploration.

PERSONALIZED LEARNING

Students create and remix STEMtells using scaffolded and personalized curriculm that increases motivation , engagment, and idenities in STEM

COMMUNITY

Students and teachers make connections to social, education, and career opportunities to the studnet created STEMtells.
